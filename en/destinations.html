Tourist Destinations

Best places in the world to visit

Mecca, Saudi Arabia

Mecca, Saudi Arabia

Enjoy the highest spirituality in the honest country until the Day of Judgment "Makkah Al-Mukarramah", which God honored with the presence of the first qiblah for Muslims, the Grand Mosque and the Kaaba. And look at the clock tower, which contains the largest clock in the world and is the second tallest tower in the world.،

Uzungöl, Turkey

Uzungöl, Turkey

There is nothing more beautiful than the views of Uzungöl in Trabzon, where the hills grow in green grasses, trees with dense leaves, and the serenity of the lake that reflects its blueness on the nature around it, so Uzungöl looks like a truly creative artist’s painting. Get ready to visit the charming Uzungol Lake in Trabzon, and do not forget to pass by the heights of Demirkapi and Fish Lake, Uzungol water cliffs, Gugit pastures, and Ataturk Museum.

Istanbul, Turkey

Istanbul, Turkey/a>

When you visit Istanbul, get ready to visit many museums, palaces, and attractions rich in antiquities and history that must be seen, such as the Hagia Sophia Museum, the Museum of Turkish Islamic Antiquities, Dolmabahce Palace, the Fatih Mosque, and the Blue Mosque.

Cappadocia, Turkey

Cappadocia, Turkey

When you visit Cappadocia, the land of balloons, prepare to cross villages carved into hillsides and rock churches for an early morning hot air balloon ride. And do not forget to visit the valleys and cities carved underground.

Land of Legends - Turkey

Land of Legends - Turkey

The land of legends in Antalya - Disneyland of Turkey - is a hotbed of legends, enchanted forests and exceptional adventures, as it contains a water city "Aqua World" that includes more than 70 water games, underwater safari tours, and escorting marine animals such as shining dolphins in fun water shows, And don't miss out on musical boat shows, fountains dancing to tunes, acrobatic performances and nightly concerts.

The Maldives

The Maldives

The Maldives is considered the most popular tourist destination for newlyweds to spend their honeymoon in the paradise of the earth, through health resorts overlooking white sandy beaches.

Dubai-United Arab Emirates

Dubai-United Arab Emirates

Perhaps you would like to live an exceptional experience by visiting the tallest building in the world "Burj Khalifa", and the third tallest building in the world "Burj Al Arab" in the charming city Dubai, the Bride of the Middle East, the city that never sleeps. And of course, you will want to visit the Bastikah neighborhood, the flower garden, Dubailand, the Dubai Desert Reserve, and the water park.

Baku, Azerbaijan (Paris of the East)

Baku, Azerbaijan (Paris of the East)

When you visit Paris of the East, Make sure to cross some of the must-see sights including Heydar Aliyev Cultural Center, Palace of the Shirvanshahanen, the famous Maiden's Tower, Enchanting Baku Beaches, Water Park, Fire Mountains, and Baku's Old City.

Batumi, Georgia

Batumi, Georgia

When you visit the charming city of Batumi, prepare to cross some of the must-see sights including Batumi Boulevard, Georgian Alphabet Tower, Batumi Dolphinarium, the strongest dolphin show, Europe Square, Botanical Garden, and cable cars.

Cairo, Egypt

Cairo, Egypt

When you visit Cairo, admire one of the Seven Wonders of the World, the Pyramids of Giza and the Great Sphinx. And visit the National Museum of Egyptian Civilization, Khan Al-Khalili, Abdeen Palace, the International Garden, and Al-Azhar Park. And do not forget to walk by the Nile at night and enjoy the charming atmosphere.

Mykonos, Greece

Mykonos, Greece

Mykonos is the island with its stunning beaches, picturesque villages and vibrant night world. Enjoy seeing the windmills, Delos Island, Delos Archaeological Museum, Gezi Castle, Chora Castle, Panagia Paraportiani Church, and the Maritime Museum.

Paris, France

Paris, France

Paris occupies a place on the list of almost every traveler. Known as the most romantic city in the world, it is home to some of the world's top attractions that are frequently featured in travel magazines and movies including the Eiffel Tower.

London, Britain

London, Britain

London the Fog, History, and Fashion City. The city of Shakespeare and Charles Dickens.. Its most famous landmarks are the Tower of London, Buckingham Palace, the Shard, and the Sea Life London complex. It's loved by who are fortunate to visit it, and dreamed by who are not lucky yet.

Rome, Italy

Rome, Italy

When you visit the Eternal City, prepare to cross some of the must-see sights - including the Colosseum, Trevi Fountain, Pantheon, St. Peter's Basilica and the Sistine Chapel in nearby Vatican City.

Giethoorn, Netherlands

Giethoorn, Netherlands

Have you ever dreamed of spending several days in a village devoid of streets and cars?! .. Giethoorn is a Dutch village similar to Venice in Italy and is applied to it (Dutch Venice) or (Venice of the East). If you want to enjoy the charming calm, picturesque nature, and clean air, and your main means of transportation is electric boats; So this is your destination.

New York, America

New York, America

When you visit the Eternal City, prepare to cross some of the must-see attractions - including the Statue of Liberty, Central Park, the Top of Rock skyscraper, the National September 11 Memorial and Museum.
